I constantly am looking for new learning opportunities to help in levelling up my skills and gaining new experiences in this vast world of programming and web development. My motivation and ambition is (1) learning and (2) applying what i have learnt in creating beautiful and functional applications that will help make people's lives easier, be a positive construct in the world and also make good money while doing so.
